 In this lesson, we're going to be talking about ads on the entertaining side of the edutainment scale. These are often the most exciting to create and the most challenging to actually convert into sales. They focus on providing the customer entertainment first and pushing your product second. The goal is to give your customer a feeling that they associate with your brand. Keep in mind, entertainment isn't just a laugh. An entertaining ad can leave you feeling inspired, shocked, excited, sad, even scared. Whatever the feeling that you want people to associate with your brand, the best way to get there is with entertaining ads. So let's get into it.
